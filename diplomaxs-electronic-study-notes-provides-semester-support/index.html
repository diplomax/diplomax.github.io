DIPLOMAX’S ELECTRONIC STUDY NOTES – PROVIDES SEMESTER SUPPORT

Nov 4th, 2014 6:39 pm

Complete your on-line DIPLOMAX registration for the January classes at www.primetimeleaning.ca and get your electronic Study Notes package IMMEDIATELY!!

Each student registered in a DIPLOMAX review class receives a complimentary copy of the Study Notes package for that course. Once you complete your registration, you can download the Study Notes package to your personal device or print your own copy.
